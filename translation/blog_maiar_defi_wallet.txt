CONFIGURE MAIAR DEFI WALLET

this tutorial I will teach you how to configure the Maiar Wallet extension!

Download the extension (Compatible: Brave / Chrome / Edge): HERE.
Added it to your browser & waited for it to launch.
Once installed click on "Create new wallet".
A pop-up appears, click on "Authorize" then "ok".
Then we will indicate the words to you (Save these words well! Otherwise you will lose your funds if you forget).
Once copied clicked on the option "I confirm I have written down and safely stores my secret phrase.".
Indicate the words requested (this is done to ensure that you have kept your seed).
Once done, create a password to unlock your wallet then click on "continue".

Well done ! you have created your Maiar defi Wallet.
