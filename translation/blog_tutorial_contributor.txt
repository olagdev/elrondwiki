HOW TO CONTRIBUTE TO ELROND WIKI

Want to contribute to Elrond Wiki ? Here are the steps:

Go to the login page
Click on the connection, you will be redirected to SAYME.

On SAYME you will only be asked for an Email, a password & to choose a free nickname.

If you don't have an account, register (It's free).
Once connected (2 validations for the first connection).
Go to the "Write an article" page.
Start writing your article.

Think in detail about your topic, especially if it concerns a project presentation.

Other than being able to write articles once logged in, you will have access to future services.
